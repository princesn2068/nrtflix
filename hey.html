"tribute to sushant singh rajput"

Late Bollywood actor Sushant Singh Rajput's death has left the entire nation in shock. Tributes from all across have been coming in since Sunday - when the news of his death first came in. From Bollywood stars to sports celebrities to policticians- all have mourned the loss of a gifted actor lik Rajput.

Now the International Space University in France has paid a rich tribute to the actor who was known to have a great interest in astronomy and quantum physics.
